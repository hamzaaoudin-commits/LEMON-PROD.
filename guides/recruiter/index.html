AI & the Recruiter.

Sourcing, screening, and outreach are becoming fully automated. The recruiters who win are the ones who can read a person and close a placement no algorithm can.

What stays irreplaceable

Reading a candidate

Sensing fit, motivation, and risk in a conversation, not a CV score.

Closing the placement

Managing the human emotion of a career move on both sides.

Trusted access

Relationships with people who aren't looking — and don't answer bots.

What AI absorbs now

Sourcing

Finding and ranking candidates at scale is automated.

Screening

First-pass filtering is increasingly a model's job.

Outreach

Initial messaging is generated and sent automatically.
