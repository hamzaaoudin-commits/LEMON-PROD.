AI & the Product Manager.

Writing specs, tickets, and status reports is automating. The PM job concentrates into its hardest core: deciding what's worth building, and what to kill.

What stays irreplaceable

Judgment on what to build

Choosing among infinite options under real constraint — the actual job.

Stakeholder alignment

Getting humans with different incentives to commit to one direction.

Saying no

Killing good ideas to protect the great one.

What AI absorbs now

Spec writing

Requirements and tickets are generated from intent.

Reporting

Status and roadmap updates are automatic.

Research synthesis

Summarizing feedback at scale is a model's job.

The hybrid move

Where to stand instead.

01

Lead with judgment

Automate documentation; sell the call on what to build.

02

Own alignment

Be the human who builds commitment, not the one who writes specs.

03

Use AI for the inputs

Let tools synthesize; you decide.

The 90-day plan

Start this quarter.

  1. 01Automate specs and reporting to focus on prioritization and alignment.
  2. 02Make your judgment on what to build your visible value.
  3. 03Sharpen the discipline of saying no.
  4. 04Stop measuring yourself in artifacts shipped; measure outcomes.

Specs write themselves now. Deciding what's worth building is the whole role.
