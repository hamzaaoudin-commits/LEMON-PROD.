AI & the Journalist.

Transcription, summarizing, and aggregation are fully automating. What no model can do is earn a source's trust, verify the truth, and put its name behind it.

What stays irreplaceable

Editorial judgment

Knowing what's actually a story, and what's noise.

Source trust

Relationships that get you what no scraper can reach.

Accountability for truth

Standing behind a claim — a model can't carry that risk.

What AI absorbs now

Transcription

Interviews are transcribed automatically.

Summarizing

Aggregation and recaps are generated free.

Routine write-ups

Formulaic reporting is automated.

The hybrid move

Where to stand instead.

01

Own original reporting

Compete on what can't be scraped — sources and verification.

02

Lead with judgment

Your editorial call is the value, not the word count.

03

Automate the grunt work

Let AI transcribe and summarize; you report and verify.

The 90-day plan

Start this quarter.

  1. 01Shift your value to original reporting and verification.
  2. 02Invest in source relationships no model can reach.
  3. 03Automate transcription and research to free time for the story.
  4. 04Build trust as your byline's competitive moat.
