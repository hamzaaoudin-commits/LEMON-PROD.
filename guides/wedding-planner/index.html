AI & the Wedding Planner.

Checklists, budgets, timelines, and vendor lists are all automating. What clients still pay for is taste, calm under pressure, and someone who makes the day perfect when it nearly isn't.

What stays irreplaceable

Taste

Designing a day that feels like the couple — not a template.

Calm under pressure

Handling the crisis at hour eleven so no one notices.

Trust

Being handed the most emotional day of someone's life.

What AI absorbs now

Logistics planning

Timelines and checklists are generated free.

Budgeting

Cost tracking is automated.

Vendor lists

Discovery and shortlists are self-serve.

The hybrid move

Where to stand instead.

01

Automate the spreadsheet

Let tools run logistics; sell the taste and the calm.

02

Own the day-of judgment

Your value is handling what goes wrong, live.

03

Be the taste

Design that feels personal is what no checklist delivers.

The 90-day plan

Start this quarter.

  1. 01Automate logistics and budgeting to focus on design and relationships.
  2. 02Make day-of crisis management your explicit, premium value.
  3. 03Build a recognizable design sensibility couples choose you for.
  4. 04Stop competing on organization; compete on taste and trust.

Checklists are free now. Taste and calm on the day are still rare.
