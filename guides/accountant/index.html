AI & the Accountant.

Bookkeeping, reconciliation, and standard reporting are collapsing toward zero marginal cost. The accountants who thrive are the ones who already moved their value up the chain.

What stays irreplaceable

Advisory judgment

Telling a business owner what the numbers mean for the decision in front of them.

Trust and discretion

Being the person a founder calls before they make a move, not after.

Regulatory accountability

Signing off on what's defensible — and carrying the responsibility for it.

What AI absorbs now

Reconciliation

Matching transactions is now an automated, exception-only task.

Standard reporting

Monthly statements and routine filings are increasingly generated, not assembled by hand.

Data entry

Capture and categorization are moving fully to software.

The hybrid move

Where to stand instead.

01

Sell forward, not backward

Stop selling the record of what happened; sell the read on what should happen next.

02

Productize advisory

Turn ad-hoc judgment into a named, priced retainer clients understand.

03

Let software do the ledger

Position automation as your tooling, and your interpretation as the product.

The 90-day plan

Start this quarter.

  1. 01Identify the 30% of your billing that is pure compliance and automate or systematize it.
  2. 02Launch one advisory retainer with a clear monthly deliverable and a real price.
  3. 03Re-introduce yourself to three clients as an advisor, not a reporter.
  4. 04Build a dashboard so 'the numbers' are a starting point for conversation, not your deliverable.

The ledger is becoming free. Your read on it is what stays scarce.
