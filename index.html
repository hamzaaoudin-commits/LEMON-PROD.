FAQ

Everything you need to decide.

Is this for me?

If you're a professional who's heard "AI will change your industry" one too many times and wants an actual answer for your specific role — yes. If you're looking for a list of ChatGPT prompts — no, that's not what this is.

Why not just read articles about AI and my industry?

Articles describe the trend. They rarely tell you what to actually do with your own career this quarter. Our guides are built to end in a decision, not just an opinion.

Is this another AI tool tutorial?

No. There's no instruction on how to use any AI tool. The guide is about strategy — what to stop competing on, what to own — independent of which AI model exists this year or next.

Will this guide still be relevant in two years?

Tool tutorials expire in months. A strategic read on what's structurally irreplaceable in your judgment, taste, or accountability ages much slower — that's the whole point of this collection.

Do you guarantee I'll keep my job?

No, and no one honest would. We give you a clear, specific picture of where your profession is headed and a concrete plan to reposition. The decision to act on it stays yours.

How do I receive the guide?

The full guide will be delivered as a PDF, built to be read in about 30 minutes. In the meantime, read the free outlook for your profession and join the waitlist to be notified when your guide ships.
